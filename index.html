What is Llamacoin?

An open-source peer-to-peer digital currency, favoured by Llamas worldwide.

At its heart, Llamacoin is the accidental crypto movement that makes people smile! It is also an opensource peer-to-peer cryptocurrency that utilises blockchain technology, a highly secure decentralised system of storing information as a public ledger that is maintained by a network of computers called nodes. More than this, though, is the ethos of Llamacoin, summarised in the Llamacoin Manifesto , and its amazing, vibrant community made up of friendly folks just like you! Learn more.
